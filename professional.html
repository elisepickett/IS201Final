Venue Tips and Tricks

  • Always try to match your venue to the vibe of the wedding.
  • Ask if they have linens that match your wedding colors.
  • If it's outdoor, make sure the venue climate-controls your space.
  • Make sure you can access their sound system with your device.

Flower Tips and Tricks

  • Think about using your bridesmaids' bouquets as centerpieces at your reception.
  • Mix it up! Use different textures and greenery to make a unique bouquet.
  • Always use your bridals as an opportunity to test your bouquet! Don't be afraid to contact your florist with corrections afterwards.

Catering Tips and Tricks

  • Read the reviews of the caterers you are considering before making your decision.
  • Ensure that they will come set up the food and take down their equipment within your receptin time frame.
  • Set up an appointment to taste test your menu!
  • Check to see if they provide cutlery and plates, this could save you a lot of money!
